What's Actually Happening

Iran and the United States have resumed indirect nuclear talks via Omani intermediaries following a breakdown in JCPOA negotiations in 2022. Iran continues to enrich uranium to 60% purity — close to weapons-grade — while maintaining it has no intent to develop nuclear weapons. US officials have indicated willingness to ease sanctions in exchange for enrichment caps. Israeli officials have publicly warned against any deal perceived as inadequate, raising the prospect of unilateral military action. The situation remains fluid with no formal framework in place as of early March 2026.
